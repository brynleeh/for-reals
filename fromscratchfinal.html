The Happiest Place on Earth!

Since I was a little girl, I've had a deep love that comes from the joy and magic that can be experienced in Disneyland. It's not only the rides, but the culture that captivates my attention and the endless memories that I have been able to create with my family and loved ones as we share in our favorite place. Disneyland has ultimately led me to appreciate the customer centric focus and illustrating magical and unforgettable experiences to last a lifetime, both for children and adults alike.
